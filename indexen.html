ABOUT US

Café Orijent has been in the catering industry for 25 years. Our services include a
rich menu from which guests choose, ketering, and Sunday lunch from 11:00 am until
15:00 pm. Orijent starts working from 07:00 am and from 09:30 am our kitchen is open where
you can order various meals and pizzas.

With many dishes we also offer various wines: black wine (Belovic), šipon (Belovic),
muškat (Kuncic), mixed wine (Kocen, Raus). Even our local bands are here to set the
mood. Café Orijent has a space that consists of a terrace for 50 people, and the
interior part of 60 seats.

If you have not been yet visited us and want to see what Orijent looks like, check out our promotional video.

WHERE CAN YOU FIND US?

The hilly end provides plenty of space for agricultural and vineyard
manufacture, and also for tourism and catering. A symbolic trademark of this scenery
is a Međimurska wine road where dozens of winemakers succeed to offer the best crops of
their own vineyards.

At the border with Slovenia, in the far north of Croatia, in Međimurje lies Štrigova,
and in the center of Štrigova you can find us! Only 19 kilometers away from Čakovec.
